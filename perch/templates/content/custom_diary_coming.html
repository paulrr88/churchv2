Instructions:

This is for the coming weeks schedule. You can add/delete items here and change the period that the schedule is for, see below.

To add a new item to the schedule: Click "Add an item" in the lower right corner underneath the bottom-most item.

To delete an item: Mouse over the number next to an item below to get a red X to appear, click the red X to delete the item.

To reorder items: Mouse over the number as above, but instead click and hold on the three lines underneath the red X and drag your mouse down/up to reorder.

As ever for any of this to take effect you need to click Save Changes at the bottom. If you make a mistake just click Cancel instead.
