Instructions:

This is for the videos page, and will allow you to add new videos.

You can either:
- Replace an existing video by changing the fields that are currently on this page
- Add a new video to the top of the list by scrolling to the bottom of this page and clicking "Save & Add another", which will result in a new blank set of fields appearing above the others that can then be filled in.

You can also delete a video by clicking the small red delete button to the top right corner of a video section.

Enter a title for the video in the Title box below (such as "Arun Sunshine Group Irish Night 2nd Nov 2013"), and then copy and paste the link to the video into the Link to video box.
