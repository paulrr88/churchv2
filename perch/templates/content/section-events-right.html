Instructions:

This is the first box in the right-hand section of the Our Regular Events page. As it may or may not be needed, this entire area can be edited or even removed as needed.

To show or hide this area on the website: Select "hidden" from the "Show/hide this area" menu below to hide everything, select "not-hidden" to show everything.

To add a section: Click "Add an item" in the lower right corner underneath the bottom-most section below.

To delete a section: Mouse over the number next to a section to get a red X to appear, click the red X to delete the section.

To reorder sections: Mouse over the number as above, but instead click and hold on the three lines underneath the red X and drag your mouse down/up to reorder.

As ever for any of this to take effect you need to click Save Changes at the bottom. If you make a mistake just click Cancel instead.
