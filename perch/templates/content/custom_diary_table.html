Instructions:

This is for the weekly diary. You can add/delete entries here and change the period that the diary is for, see below.

To add a new diary entry: Click "Add an item" in the lower right corner underneath the bottom-most diary entry.

To delete a diary entry: Mouse over the number next to a diary entry below to get a red X to appear, click the red X to delete the entry.

To reorder diary entries: Mouse over the number as above, but instead click and hold on the three lines underneath the red X and drag your mouse down/up to reorder.

As ever for any of this to take effect you need to click Save Changes at the bottom. If you make a mistake just click Cancel instead.
